About the band

Margaret Glaspy is an American singer-songwriter from Sacramento California. Her first EP HomeSchool was released in 2012. She signed to ATO records in 2015 and released You and I and Somebody to Anybody which were later included in her 2016 album Emotions and Math In March 2020 she released her second full-length album Devotion.
